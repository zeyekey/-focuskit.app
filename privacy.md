# FocusKit - Privacy Policy

[cite_start]The FocusKit mobile application ("the App") is a premium, minimalist, and behavior-oriented digital ritual assistant designed to work alongside the physical FocusKit product set. We value your privacy immensely and process your data solely to deliver the core features of the App.

### 1. Data Collected and Purposes of Use
[cite_start]The following data is collected within the App and stored securely using our Firebase infrastructure[cite: 482, 483]:
* [cite_start]**Email Address:** Used for user registration, account authentication, and synchronizing your premium package across devices[cite: 83, 86, 97].
* [cite_start]**Focus Session Data:** The duration of your focus slots, session titles, phone parking completion, and screen break statistics are recorded to provide insights on your "Progress" screen[cite: 520, 521, 522, 523, 524, 525, 526, 527, 528, 529].
* [cite_start]**Device and Analytical Data:** Anonymous crash reports and basic app performance metrics are tracked via Firebase Analytics to ensure structural stability[cite: 486, 551].
* [cite_start]**Notification Tokens (FCM Token):** Used to send you gentle daily focus reminders, morning reset alerts, and evening shutdown ritual prompts[cite: 364, 366, 368, 370, 485].
* [cite_start]**Camera Access:** Requested strictly on-demand if you choose to scan the Premium QR card included in your box to activate your tier (Starter, Signature, or Corporate)[cite: 51, 71, 87, 88].

### 2. Strict Privacy Guarantee for Corporate Users (FocusKit Corporate)
[cite_start]If you are using the App through your employer (Corporate tier), your individual focus titles, daily 3 focuses, notes, and specific session habits are strictly confidential and will never be shown to your company[cite: 78, 329]. [cite_start]The corporate dashboard only displays aggregate, anonymous, team-level participation data to protect your workplace peace[cite: 75, 78, 314, 322].
