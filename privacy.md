# FocusKit — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

The FocusKit mobile application ("the App") is a premium, minimalist, and behavior-oriented digital ritual assistant designed to work alongside the physical FocusKit product set. We value your privacy and process your data solely to deliver the core features of the App.

## 1. Data Collected and Purposes of Use

The following data is collected within the App and stored securely using our Firebase infrastructure:

- **Email Address:** Used for user registration, account authentication, and synchronizing your premium package across devices.
- **Focus Session Data:** The duration of your focus slots, session titles, phone parking completion, and screen break statistics are recorded to provide insights on your "Progress" screen.
- **Device and Analytical Data:** Anonymous crash reports and basic app performance metrics are tracked via Firebase Analytics to ensure structural stability.
- **Notification Tokens (FCM Token):** Used to send you gentle daily focus reminders, morning reset alerts, and evening shutdown ritual prompts.
- **Camera Access:** Requested strictly on-demand if you choose to scan the Premium QR card included in your box to activate your tier (Starter, Signature, or Corporate).

## 2. Strict Privacy Guarantee for Corporate Users (FocusKit Corporate)

If you are using the App through your employer (Corporate tier), your individual focus titles, daily focuses, notes, and specific session habits are strictly confidential and will never be shown to your company. The corporate dashboard only displays aggregate, anonymous, team-level participation data to protect your workplace peace.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request full deletion of your account and associated data at any time. See our [Account Deletion Policy](account-deletion.md) for instructions.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (Google) for authentication, data storage, analytics, and push notifications. Firebase's own privacy practices are described at [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

## 5. Children's Privacy

FocusKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
