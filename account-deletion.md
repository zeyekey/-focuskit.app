# FocusKit — Account Deletion

_Last updated: June 2026_

You can permanently delete your FocusKit account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how, and what happens when you do.

## How to delete your account

1. Open the FocusKit app on your device.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

- Your email address and authentication credentials
- All Focus Session records (titles, durations, completion status)
- Daily Plans and Weekly Plans
- Shutdown Ritual entries and history
- Notification tokens (FCM)
- Premium package / activation status linked to your account

## What is retained

- Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot be tied back to your individual account (e.g. overall app usage statistics) may be retained for product improvement purposes.
- If you are part of a FocusKit Corporate team, anonymized team-level aggregate participation data (which never included your individual data) is unaffected by your personal account deletion.

## Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app (e.g. you uninstalled it or lost your login credentials) but want to request account deletion, email us at:

- **focuskit.app@gmail.com**

Please include the email address associated with your account so we can locate and delete your data. We will confirm once the deletion is complete.
