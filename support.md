# FocusKit — Support & FAQ

Experiencing an issue with your FocusKit ritual? Check our frequently asked questions below, or reach out directly to our support team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### My QR code / activation code from the box is not working. What should I do?

Please ensure that the activation code on the card inside your physical kit has not been scanned previously. If you encounter a verification error, please email us at **focuskit.app@gmail.com** along with a photo of your box or receipt, and we will issue a replacement code right away.

### Does the focus timer stop if I lock my phone or push the App to the background?

No. FocusKit is intentionally designed to let you park your phone away. If you lock your device or switch apps, our background system tracks the duration seamlessly. When you return to the App, your elapsed focus progress will automatically calculate and update without losing your session data.

### Can my employer see my daily focuses, small wins, or personalized notes?

Absolutely not. Your daily focuses, notes, distractions list, and personal logs are completely private to your individual account. Your company dashboard only aggregates anonymous data (e.g., total focused minutes across the team) to evaluate general engagement without ever tracking individual performance.

### How do I delete my account?

Open the App, go to **Settings → Delete Account**, and confirm. This permanently removes your account and associated data. See our [Account Deletion Policy](account-deletion.md) for full details.

### I lost access to my physical kit's activation card. Can I still get premium?

Yes — email us with a photo of your box or original receipt and we'll issue a replacement activation code.
