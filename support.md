# FocusKit - Support & FAQ

Experiencing an issue with your FocusKit ritual? Check our frequently asked questions below, or reach out directly to our support team.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

#### 1. My QR code / Activation code from the box is not working. What should I do?
[cite_start]Please ensure that the activation code on the card inside your physical kit has not been scanned previously[cite: 95]. If you encounter a verification error, please email us at **focuskit.app@gmail.com** along with a photo of your box or receipt, and we will issue a replacement code right away.

#### 2. Does the focus timer stop if I lock my phone or push the App to the background?
[cite_start]No. FocusKit is intentionally designed to let you park your phone away[cite: 5, 14, 151]. [cite_start]If you lock your device or switch apps, our background system tracks the duration seamlessly[cite: 203]. [cite_start]When you return to the App, your elapsed focus progress will automatically calculate and update without losing your session data[cite: 204, 605, 609].

#### 3. Can my employer see my daily focuses, small wins, or personalized notes?
Absolutely not. [cite_start]Your daily 3 focuses, notes, distractions list, and personal logs are completely private to your individual account[cite: 78, 329]. [cite_start]Your company dashboard only aggregates anonymous data (e.g., total focused minutes across the team) to evaluate general engagement without ever tracking individual performance[cite: 75, 78, 314].
